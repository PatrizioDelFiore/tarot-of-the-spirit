This tarot deck is used differently from all others and has the unique characteristic of being a “self-reading” deck. It requires no knowledge of how tarot works and does not include any accompanying “manual.”

You simply place the cards in front of you, bring your hands together at the center of your chest in a gesture of inner reflection, and mentally ask a question. Then you draw one card… then another… as long as you still have questions.

Happy reading to everyone from Patrizio Fiorenza!
