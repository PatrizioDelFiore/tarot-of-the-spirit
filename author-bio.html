Author Bio

My name is Patrizio Fiorenza, born in Rome on July 8th, 1990. Since childhood, I have been drawn to spirituality—an inner curiosity that has accompanied me quietly but steadily through every stage of my life.

Along with this sensitivity, I have always felt a natural attraction toward art and literature. Yet life led me down a different professional path: working at the Bank of Italy as a statistician. Although this world may seem far from spiritual exploration, it gifted me discipline, clarity, and a way of observing patterns—skills that later became essential to my creative and inner journey.

Today, I try to bring together both sides of my nature. I use the analytical tools and precision learned in my professional life to share, with sincerity and dedication, the spiritual texts and experiences that have shaped me. At the same time, I am reclaiming the artistic and expressive dimension that has always belonged to me.

Tarot of the Spirit is one of the spaces where these two worlds meet: intuition and structure, inspiration and method, coming together with a single purpose — to illuminate, uplift, and guide.
